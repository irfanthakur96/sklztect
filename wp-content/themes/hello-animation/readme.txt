Hello Animation comes with a library of starter templates, enabling users to quickly import pre-built designs.

## Description

Hello Animation offers an extensive collection of starter templates, designed to help users effortlessly build their websites with just a few clicks. These pre-built templates cater to a wide range of industries and website types, including blogs, portfolios, business sites, and more. Each template is carefully crafted to maintain a professional and modern design, ensuring that your website looks polished and ready for launch.

With the easy import feature, users can select from a variety of layouts and design styles, eliminating the need to start from scratch. This not only saves time but also provides a solid foundation for customization. Whether you’re a beginner looking for a quick setup or an experienced developer seeking a head start, these templates give you the flexibility to fine-tune the design to match your branding and vision.

In addition, the starter templates are fully responsive and optimized for SEO, ensuring that your site performs well across all devices and ranks favorably in search engine results. By leveraging these ready-made designs, users can create stunning websites with minimal effort, while still having the ability to customize every detail to their liking.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

= Does this theme support any plugins? =
